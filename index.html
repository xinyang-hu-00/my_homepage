About

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics & Data Science at Yale University (advised by Prof. Zhuoran Yang), with research interests in statistical machine learning, foundations of LLMs, AI agents, and responsible & interpretable AI. Before Yale, I completed a B.S. in Mathematics (summa cum laude) with a Computer Science minor at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

My recent work spans multi-agent systems for scientific discovery and negotiation, theoretical foundations of in-context and chain-of-thought learning, and scalable infrastructure for training and evaluating LLMs.

HYPA: Hybrid Pathways Agent · Developer · Spring 2025
